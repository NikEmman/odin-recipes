Lasagna recipe

Description :

Lasagna is an Italian dish, well beloved by all the world. It is the favorite food of Garfield, a lovely chubby orange cat cartoon.

It consist of pasta leafs and tomato sause mixture between. On top there is cheese. Much cheese.

Ingredients :

  • Lasagna leafs
  • 500 ml tomato sauce
  • 300 gr Motsarella cheese
  • 200 gr mushrooms
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 100 ml fresh milk
  • 300 ml water

Steps:

  1. Get a big oven pan
  2. Put a level of lasagna leafs
  3. Mix tomato sause with mushrooms, salt, pepper
  4. Spread the mixture on top of first lasagna layer
  5. Get a second lasagna layer, and repeat previous step, until you have 3 layers of tomato mixture and 4 lasagna
  6. SPread the cheese love on top
  7. Poor milk and water in the pan
  8. Pre-heat oven at 180 C, bake food for 40 mins
